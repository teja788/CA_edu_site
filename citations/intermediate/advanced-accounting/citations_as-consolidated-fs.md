# Citations — Intermediate P1 Ch 10: Accounting Standards for Consolidated Financial Statements

Every concept cited in the notes and question bank for this chapter (AS 21,
AS 23, AS 27), with the primary-source basis a reviewer should spot-check.
Positions are stated in original words, with deliberate near-verbatim
exceptions (marked ※ below) where exam answers require the
definitional/operative wording: the AS 21 control definition ("ownership,
directly or indirectly through subsidiary(ies), of more than one-half of the
voting power" / "control of the composition of the board of directors so as
to obtain economic benefits"), the AS 21 exclusion wording ("acquired and
held exclusively with a view to its subsequent disposal in the near future"
and "severe long-term restrictions which significantly impair its ability to
transfer funds to the parent"), the AS 23 significant-influence definition
("power to participate in the financial and/or operating policy decisions of
the investee but not control over those policies") and the AS 27 joint-control
definition ("contractually agreed sharing of control over an economic
activity"). Everything else: original prose vs SM Ch 10 Units 1–3.

| SM Ch 10 U3 (AS 27) ※ | Joint venture / joint control / venturer / investor definitions; contractual arrangement (usually written — contract, minutes, articles) distinguishes JV from associate; no single venturer controls unilaterally | notes §3; q-030, q-037; d-007, d-008 |
| SM Ch 10 U3 (AS 27) | Three forms — JCO: venturer recognises assets it controls, liabilities/expenses it incurs, share of income (same in separate and consolidated FS); JCA: share of joint assets classified by NATURE of the asset, liabilities incurred, share of joint liabilities, share of output/income with share of expenses | notes §3; q-031, q-032, q-033; d-007 |
| SM Ch 10 U3 (AS 27) | JCE: separate FS → AS 13 investment; CFS → proportionate consolidation (venturer's share of each asset, liability, income, expense, line by line or as separate line items, gross, no offsetting); no CFS without subsidiaries → no proportionate consolidation; exemptions (near-future disposal, severe long-term restrictions → AS 13); cease when joint control ends (then AS 21/23/13) | notes §3 + WE5; q-034, q-035, q-036; d-007; cs-004 |

| SM Ch 10 U3 (AS 27) | Investor in a JV (no joint control): AS 13, or AS 21/AS 23 where control/significant influence in fact exists; operators'/managers' fees = revenue per AS 9 (expense of the venture); disclosures: contingent liabilities and capital commitments separately, list of JVs with interest proportions, aggregate current/long-term assets and liabilities, income and expenses of JCE interests | notes §3; q-037; d-008 |
