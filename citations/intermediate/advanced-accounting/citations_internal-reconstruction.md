## Numerical conventions used (not statutory citations)

- MCQ/worked-example arithmetic verified by
  `scripts/verify_numerical/verify_internal-reconstruction.py` (25/25 pass,
  this session); blind consistency pass 45/45 agreed (0 mismatches).
- Entry-selection MCQs whose options share a rupee figure (q-011, q-013,
  q-023, q-025) are deliberately numerical:false — the distinguishing factor
  is the account, not the arithmetic; the blind pass covers them.
- Debenture-holder sacrifice measured on principal only; a coupon change
  (12% → 10%) affects future interest, not the reconstruction credit — noted
  in q-010's explanation.
- Surrender arithmetic: sacrifice = claim − value of shares re-allotted;
  cancellation credit = surrendered − re-allotted, at paid-up value.
