# Citations — Intermediate P1 Ch 9: Other Accounting Standards

Every concept cited in the notes, question bank and flashcards for this
chapter (AS 12, AS 14 standard text), with the primary-source basis a
reviewer should spot-check. AS 14's working mechanics (merger/purchase
conditions, purchase consideration, pooling/purchase entries, statutory
reserves) are covered — and cited — in Ch 13 Amalgamation of Companies;
this chapter deliberately covers only the standard-text angles absent
there. Positions are stated in original words, with deliberate
near-verbatim exceptions (marked ※ below) where exam answers require the
definitional/operative wording: the AS 12 grant-definition head
("assistance by government in cash or kind"), the Method II release rule
("on a systematic and rational basis over the useful life"), the
nominal-value trigger ("where the grant equals the whole, or virtually the
whole"), AS 14's acquisition-scope sentence ("the acquired company is not
dissolved and its separate entity continues to exist"), the five-year
goodwill rule ("a period not exceeding five years unless a somewhat longer
period can be justified"), the goodwill factor "service-life expectancies
of key individuals or groups of employees", and the AS 14 disclosure-list
items (names and general nature of business; percentage of each company's
equity shares exchanged). All 23 surviving 10-word shingles in the notes
and 8 in the bank sit inside those clusters; flashcards: zero overlap.
Everything else: zero overlap vs SM Ch 9 Units 1–2.
