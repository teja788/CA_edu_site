# Citations — Intermediate P1 Ch 6: Liabilities Based Accounting Standards

Every concept cited in the notes, question bank and flashcards for this
chapter (AS 15, AS 29), with the primary-source basis a reviewer should
spot-check. Positions are stated in original words, with deliberate
near-verbatim exceptions (marked ※ below) where exam answers require the
definitional/operative wording: the AS 15 short-term-benefit definition
("fall due wholly within twelve months after the end of the period in which
the service is rendered"), the AS 29 definitions block (provision; liability;
the contingent-liability and contingent-asset "confirmed only by the
occurrence or non-occurrence of one or more uncertain future events" limbs),
AS 29's best-estimate measurement rule ("best estimate of the expenditure
required to settle the present obligation at the balance sheet date"), and
AS 29's discount-rate wording ("current market assessments of the time value
of money and the risks specific to the liability"). All 105 surviving
10-word shingles in the notes and 6 in the bank sit inside those clusters;
flashcards: zero overlap. Everything else: zero overlap vs SM Ch 6 Units 1–2.
