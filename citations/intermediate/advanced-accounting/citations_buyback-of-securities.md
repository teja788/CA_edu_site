## Numerical conventions used (not statutory citations)

- MCQ/worked-example arithmetic verified by `scripts/verify_numerical/verify_buyback-of-securities.py` (26/26 pass, this session).
- Premium on buyback written off against securities premium first, then free
  reserves — the standard ICAI-solution sequencing; the Act itself does not
  prescribe an order. Stated as an accounting convention, not law.
- Debt-equity test solved as: maximum outflow = shareholders' funds − debt/2.
  This is the SM's standard simplification (it ignores second-order CRR
  effects); flagged for reviewer awareness.
